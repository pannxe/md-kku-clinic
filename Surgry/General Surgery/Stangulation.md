# Cardinal Signs
- Fever
- Tachycardia
- Leukocytosis
- Local abdominal pain

if all 4 present → 2/3 chance of stangulation, also:
- Colicky → **const. dull pain**
- **[[Peritonitis]]**
- **High-pitch hyperactive** bowel sound & **borborygmi**
- DRE → mucous bloody stool (prev. empty ← obs.)
